About Us

We are a well-established Athlone-based computer sales and services business that has been in operation since September 2000. Our retail shop and computer repair centre on Abbey Street has become a busy destination for customers who wish to purchase the latest technology or to have their computer serviced by qualified technicians.

We have highly experienced staff can also offer sales advice to small businesses owners and provide IT support to them so that their systems are kept running at their optimum.

Our Story

We can offer a wide range of products and services. Whether you need to purchase ink for your home printer, or a server computer for your business. Whether you need get a laptop repaired or a computer network designed. It is ultimately our goal to offer a one-stop facility for all home and business IT needs, including both sales and servicing. In this way we can offer greater perceived value for the customer than many other local shops who only either offer sales or services. Although the retail consumer electronics market is highly competitive, we can offer value added service to all of the goods that we have to offer you, from purchase advice through to demonstration and support. We dont see our service offering as just a commodity, it is what makes us outstanding in the face of our competitors.

Services

We have qualified technicians on hand to fix your problem. Our expertise is second to none and unlike other repair shops we will fix the problem and not just reset your machine. Our customer care starts the second you walk in the door and we will talk to you in a language that you will understand. Every repair is logged into our system where-after you will receive a job reference number by SMS to your phone. Our rates are reasonable as we believe that you pay for what you get.
